Controls
- AD for left and right motion
- W for jump
- Space to Trigger Mace Jump (The Yellow & White Orbs are special boosts that by pressing space can make you jump really high)
- Shift to Trigger Spear Dash (Pressing Shift will make you dash in the direction you are facing)

Levels
- Level 1 > Sneh Shihora
> Unique Mechanic: Platforms you can jump on from below
- Level 2 > Julian Tran
> Unique Mechanic: Mace Orbs you can boost off of
- Pressing ‘G’ can help you switch between levels

Bonus Elements
- Platforms that you can jump up from below.
- Yellow/White Orbs that act as special boosts for jumping
- Dashing Mechanic
- Yellow Diamond Collectibles

Camera
- The Camera is a side scroller and just follows the player
